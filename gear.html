Camera

Lenses

I have a Sony a6000 which is an ok camera but i would not recommend buying it now because for its age the price has not really decreased that much. Generally the camera body doesn't make that much of a difference compared to the lens. It might be easier to take better pictures with a cheaper camera because it's simpler to get over the fear of breaking it. Good pictures don't come from the best specs but lots of trial and error and practice which you can get if you aren't scared to just throw the camera into your bag and have it with you all the time.

Usually I have a pancake lens on it s.t. it fits into a pocket and is easily accessible. Most of the time I also have one more lens (the fish eye or 50 mm f1.4 prime) in my back for if i want to use it.
